MY BIOGRAPHY

Robin was born in New York City, and raised in the tri-state area of NY, NJ, and PA. He graduated from Lehigh University with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering, and moved to Vancouver WA where he worked as a development engineer for Hewlett-Packard. He's had jobs ranging from designing circuit boards in R&D to developing test applications in manufacturing and IT. He received a B.S. in Computer Science at Portland State University (2015), and an AAT in Web Development at Clark College (2018). He now develops applications for desktop, web, and mobile.
